Spring Watch

Here are some great places to visit this Spring.

Beautiful Bluebells

The secret is out! Hucking Estate, not far from Maidstone, is a stunning location to find this years Bluebells in their full glory. With the woodland floor carpeted in Bluebells and other wildflowers you will be itching to identify all the wonderful flora here.

Our Website
Woodland Trust

Sunny Sandpits!

For the best sandpit in the South East you can't beat Grays Beach Riverside Park. It has climbing frames for all ages and abilities and is sure to be a big hit. Only 3 miles from Lakeside Shopping Centre it's perfect when the little ones need to run off some steam.

Our Website

Somewhere Indoors

The Beaney House of Art and Knowledge is a treasure trove in Canterbury's City Centre. This lovely museum offers lots for its smallest visitors with wonderful Explorer Points for the Children to investigate and BFG inspired activities, as the museum displays some of Quentin Blake's artwork.
