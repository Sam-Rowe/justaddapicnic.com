Autumn Colours

Here are some great places to visit this Autumn.

Shorne Woods Country Park

Autumn is all about muddy puddles, wellies and spectacular natural colours. At Shorne Woods Country Park you'll fnd it all. Explore and discover fungi, nuts and seeds in amoungst the woodland.

Bonkers for Conkers!

Sit in Victoria Gardens and enjoy a picnic looking over the Medway River. Play in the little park and then see if you can find some conkers. Just a few minutes walk from Chatham train station.

Escape from the Weather
