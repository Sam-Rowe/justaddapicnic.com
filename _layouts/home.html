Summer Sun

Here are some great places to visit this Summer.

Herne Bay

This classic seaside town has so much to offer all year round. When the weather is particularly good why not enjoy a splash in the sea, crabbing off the pier, a play in the park and then fish and chips on the beach as the sun starts to go down. Easy to access via road and rail.

Sunny Sandpits!

For the best sandpit in the South East you can't beat Grays Beach Riverside Park. It has climbing frames for all ages and abilities and is sure to be a big hit. Only 3 miles from Lakeside Shopping Centre it's perfect when the little ones need to run off some steam.

Our Website

Folkestone Fun

Folkestone sea front has undergone a massive revamp in the past few years. The Lower Leas Coastal park offers a wonderful place to play once you've explored Sunny Sands beach, the trendy Harbour Arm and stone beach along Marine Parade. Explore Folkestone Museum too.
